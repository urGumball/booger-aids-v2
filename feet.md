# Feet

Feet are like your personal comedians: always down to earth, but rarely taken seriously.

- 26 bones per foot means they are basically little skeleton parties.
- They carry you through life, step by step, one awkward sock at a time.
- Feet are the original walking puns: always a little *toe-tally* in charge.

Whether you're barefoot, in sneakers, or stuck in slippers, your feet deserve a round of applause ... or at least a good pair of socks.

> "If life is a journey, then feet are the unsung heroes doing all the heavy lifting."

Never underestimate the power of a good foot joke. After all, they really know how to put their best foot forward.
